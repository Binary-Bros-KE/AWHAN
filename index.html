Empowering Women for a Better Tomorrow

AWHAN is dedicated to fostering an environment where women's contributions are recognized and celebrated in the ASAL region of Kenya.

We aim to strengthen the capabilities of women-led organizations, provide resources for women-driven initiatives, and advocate for social change. Our work is grounded in the belief that empowering women unlocks potential and drives sustainable development across the region.

Our Focus Areas

Empowering Communities through Education, Health, and Advocacy

With a deep commitment to improving lives in northern Kenya, we focus on strategic goals that make a lasting impact in education, health, livelihood, peace building, and advocacy for marginalized communities.

Our Focus Areas:
  • Education: Improving access, enrolment, and performance in quality education.
  • Livelihood Programs: Reducing poverty through diversified projects, increasing access to clean water, and empowering youth and women.
  • Health: Fighting diseases like malaria and HIV/AIDS, reducing FGM, promoting family planning, and combating drug abuse.
  • Peace Building: Strengthening conflict resolution methods in pastoral communities through ADRM initiatives.
  • Advocacy: Promoting human rights, civic education, and supporting vulnerable groups, including women and children.
Our Organization

Vision, Mission & Goal

Our Vision

Promote an inclusive society where the voices of women are not only heard but actively considered in economic and social development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to strengthen women-led organizations and amplify the voices of women who are catalysts for positive change within their communities.

Our Goal

To advocate for the inclusion of women, ensuring they gain access to their social and economic rights.

Our Objectives

The Core Objectives of Our Organization

We are committed to promoting advocacy, fostering peace, and improving livelihoods in marginalized communities.

Advocacy

We advocate for girl child education, inclusivity in policy formulation, good governance, health rights, and accountability. We also campaign against harmful practices such as FGM and early marriages.

Peace Building

We promote community cohesion, integration, women empowerment, and cross-border peace initiatives. We also work on countering violent extremism and fostering conflict resolution.

Livelihood Activities

Our livelihood programs focus on providing clean water, sanitation, electricity, and healthcare services to improve the quality of life in underprivileged areas.
