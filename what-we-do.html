Our Focus Areas

Empowering Communities through Education, Health, and Advocacy

With a deep commitment to improving lives in northern Kenya, we focus on strategic goals that make a lasting impact in education, health, livelihood, peace building, and advocacy for marginalized communities.

Our Focus Areas:
  • Education: Improving access, enrolment, and performance in quality education.
  • Livelihood Programs: Reducing poverty through diversified projects, increasing access to clean water, and empowering youth and women.
  • Health: Fighting diseases like malaria and HIV/AIDS, reducing FGM, promoting family planning, and combating drug abuse.
  • Peace Building: Strengthening conflict resolution methods in pastoral communities through ADRM initiatives.
  • Advocacy: Promoting human rights, civic education, and supporting vulnerable groups, including women and children.

AWHAN
